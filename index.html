Foreword

On this website, several literary works from different time periods will be analyzed. These literary works and time periods include:

-The Middle Ages (The Canon's Yeoman's Tale; Chaucer, The Cock and the Fox; Robert Henryson)

-The Rennaisance (Sonnet 24; Shakespeare, The Good-Morrow; John Donne)

-The Enlightenment (On His Blindness; John Milton, Epitaph on a Child; Thomas Grey)

-The Romantic Period (The Raven; Edgar Allen Poe, The Masque of the Ref Death; Edgar Allen Poe)

-The Victorian Period (How do I Love Thee?; Elisabeth Barret Browning, The Yellow Wallpaper; Charlotte Perkins Gilman)

On this website there is also an original short story, written for this project.

We have created this website for educational purposes, as a project for our studies in Cambridge English.
